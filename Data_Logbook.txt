   Base stake range: 100-1000 units
   Initial reputation: 0.5
   Consensus threshold: 2/3 weighted votes
   Aggregation threshold: n/2 + 1 participants

----------------------------------------------------------------------
DATA COLLECTION NOTES
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Observation 1: Consensus latency scales sub-quadratically
   Expected O(n²) from literature
   Observed O(n^1.8) with batch voting optimization
   Statistical significance: p < 0.001

Observation 2: Byzantine threshold strictly enforced
   Success rate >95% when f < n/3
   Success rate drops to ~70% when f ≥ n/3
   Matches theoretical predictions

Observation 3: Privacy mechanisms show consistent efficiency
   Laplace: 97% budget efficiency
   Gaussian: 94% budget efficiency
   Both exceed 90% threshold for practical use

Observation 4: Aggregation scales linearly with dimension
   O(d^1.02) complexity measured
   Confirms theoretical O(d) prediction
   Suitable for high-dimensional federated learning
